# Brokers-of-NAM
Brokers-of-NAM analyzes the history of the Non-Aligned Movement (NAM) through the lens of transnational political networks and relying on an innovative methodological approach combining biographical and network analysis. 

## Overview of the project
The project analyzes the transnational networks of the Non-aligned Movement through structured data coming from the study of five biographies. 

The sources of the project are various mainly archival, print, press, and photographic. Different strategies are employed to read and exploit those sources, combining both the use of tools for distant reading with the traditional close reading of documents.

The project aims to preserve openness, both in terms of the data as in terms of its general process of organization. This reposity will make available different versions of the data all throughout the project with the purpose of showng the progress of the research in its different phases.
